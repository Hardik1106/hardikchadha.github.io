About Me

I am from Jalandhar, Punjab and have completed my schooling from Innocent Hearts School, Green Model Town, Jalandhar.
I am currently pursuing my B.Tech and M.S in Computer Science and Engineering at the International Institute of Information Technology (IIITH).
In addition to my academic pursuits, I am interested in sports and have played Table Tennis at the National Level.
I am currently involved in research at the Software Engineering Research Center (SERC) Lab, IIIT Hyderabad, under the guidance of Prof. Abhishek Kr Singh. My work focuses on Trustworthy Automated Programming, aiming to reduce ambiguity in AI-generated code by aligning natural language intent with formal specifications.
